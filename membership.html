GT Glee Club Alumni Association

Membership

We strive to provide our members with as many ways to connect with each other and the club as possible. Whether it’s discord chatter, concert live-streams, or our annual Reunion Dinner during GT Homecoming Weekend, our goal continues to focus on building and maintaining friendships over our common bond of music.

Current members: 33

Scholarship

Not only do we take pride in bringing together Georgia Tech Glee Club alumni across generations, but we’ve also had the pleasure of furthering our mission of supporting the arts by awarding academic scholarships to current Glee Club members.

Scholarships given: $1000

Graduating Years

The GTGCAA already includes members from fifteen different graduating classes since the club’s founding in 1906. As we grow through the years, we’re committed to connecting as many of the thousands of alumni as possible.

Classes represented: 15

States

Although we all gathered in Atlanta, GA for our time at Georgia Tech, our members reside across the entire country. From Florida to Washington or Utah to Illinois, our organization aims to connect our alumni through in-person and online events.

States represented: 14
