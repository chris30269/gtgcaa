The Scholarship

We are so excited to announce the scholarship winners for the 2021-2022 school year! Thanks to the generosity of our members and outside donors, we were able to award two $500 scholarships to two exceptional students in the Georgia Tech Glee Club. Both winners will be attending the annual GT Glee Club Alumni Association Dinner during GT Homecoming Weekend where they will be recognized for their achievement.

The Winners

Joonha Hwang

GTGCAA Scholarship Recipient

Joonha is the current president of the Glee Club and has been a member for 2 years.

Andrew Tillery

Crawford Family Scholarship Recipient

Andrew is the current manager of the Glee Club and has been a member for 4 years.
