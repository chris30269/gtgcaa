The Scholarship

We are so excited to announce the scholarship winners for the 2021-2022 school year! Thanks to the generosity of our members and outside donors, we were able to award two $500 scholarships to two exceptional students in the Georgia Tech Glee Club. Both winners will be attending the annual GT Glee Club Alumni Association Dinner during GT Homecoming Weekend where they will be recognized for their achievement.

The Winners

Joonha Hwang

GTGCAA Scholarship Recipient

A second year mechanical engineering major from Korea, Joonha Hwang currently serves as the President of the Glee Club. Believe it or not, Tech students do have hobbies outside of school, and Joonha is no different. He enjoys solar racing, robotics, mario kart, sleeping at a reasonable hour, and arranging bass melodies. His favorite glee club arrangement is “Twist and La Bamba” and he would like for everyone to know that his high school did not have a choir, so he was forced to learn how to sing in the shower.


Congratulations, Joonha!

Andrew Tillery

Crawford Family Scholarship Recipient

Our Crawford Family Scholarship winner is a fourth year computer engineering major from Philadelphia, PA. Andrew enjoys climbing and hiking in his free time as well as shimmying as much as he can when the Glee Club performs “Twist and La Bamba”. His favorite gig is our annual concert at Spivey Hall. An interesting fact about him: Andrew has ridden over 110 roller coasters in his life. We’re so proud to have watched Andrew grow as a performer and as a person over these last four years.


Congratulations, Andrew!
