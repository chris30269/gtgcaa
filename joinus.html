Join Us

Interested in joining? We’d love to have you as a part of our budding network of Georgia Tech Glee Club alumni. We only require you to have been a member of the club for at least one academic semester.


To join, fill out the form below and donate at least $65 in any of the following ways:

  • Venmo: @GTGCAA with your name in the description of the payment.
  • PayPal: Donate to GTGCAA
  • Benevity: GT Glee Club Alumni Association
  • Mail a check: Make out to “GT Glee Club Alumni Association Inc.” and mail to:
    • 172 Haynes St SW Unit 116, Atlanta, GA 30313
