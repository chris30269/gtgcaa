Founded in 2021, the GT Glee Club Alumni Association Inc. is the official alumni organization of the Georgia Tech Glee Club. We gladly represent thousands of alumni from across the world who all had the pleasure of being a part of such a wonderful organization of music and performance in their time at Georgia Tech. As a non-profit and 501(c)3, our goals not only consist of growing and nurturing such a strong alumni network, but we are also dedicated towards giving back to the students who still bring the Glee Club music alive to this day.
